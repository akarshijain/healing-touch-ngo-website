Dr Puneet Agarwal

About Our Founder

Dr. Puneet Agarwal, an exemplary medical professional, is the Senior Director of Stroke and Dementia Neurosciences at Neurology Max Super Speciality Hospital, Saket. His journey is marked by not only his exceptional contributions to the field of neurosciences but also his deep commitment to serving humanity through his transformative vision.

With an illustrious career spanning decades, Dr. Agarwal has seamlessly woven together his profound medical expertise with an unyielding dedication to making a meaningful impact on society. Beyond his role as a medical practitioner, Dr. Agarwal stands as the visionary founder of a remarkable non-governmental organization (NGO) that echoes his resolute ambition and compassionate spirit.

Driven by an unwavering belief in the power of healthcare to change lives, Dr. Agarwal's NGO is a testament to his dedication and goals. Through this noble endeavor, he seeks to bridge the gap between medical excellence and the marginalized segments of our community. His mission transcends medical treatment, aiming to empower the underprivileged with access to healthcare services, knowledge, and essential resources.

Dr. Agarwal's motivation for his charitable work is rooted in a deeply held conviction that every individual deserves the right to quality healthcare. His compassion and empathy fuel his tireless efforts to extend a helping hand to those who need it most.
